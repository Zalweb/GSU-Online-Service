About GSU

Learn more about the General Services Unit and our mission

Who We Are

The General Services Unit (GSU) is responsible for the maintenance, upkeep, and overall management of university facilities and services. We ensure that the campus environment is safe, clean, and conducive to learning and working.

Our Mission

To provide efficient, reliable, and high-quality general services that support the academic, administrative, and operational needs of the university community. We are committed to maintaining a well-functioning campus through timely and professional service delivery.

Services We Offer

  • Venue Reservation – Booking of halls, conference rooms, and open spaces for events
  • Transportation – Vehicle requests for official trips, fieldwork, and university activities
  • Carpentry Works – Furniture repair, installation, and custom woodwork
  • Aircon Maintenance – AC cleaning, repair, and installation services
  • Electrical Maintenance – Wiring, lighting, and power system repairs
  • Janitorial Services – Campus cleaning, sanitation, and waste management

How It Works

Our online request system allows students, faculty, and staff to submit service requests conveniently from any device, anytime. Simply fill out the online request form, provide the necessary details, and our team will review and process your request promptly.

Each request is tracked and managed through our admin dashboard, ensuring that no request goes unnoticed and all services are delivered efficiently.
